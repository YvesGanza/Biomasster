About Us

BioMassters Ltd. is a wood pellet clean cooking company operating in Rwanda that places customer centricity at the heart of its operations while tackling 7 SDGs at the same time. It runs an innovative business model, based on PAYGO principles, to make sure the majority of the urban Rwandan population can access its services: a tier 4 gasifier stove and wood pellets as everyday cooking fuel.

Fast and easy - be cooking in just one to two minutes
Costs less - customers save 30%+ compared to dirty charcoal
Available to everyone - no matter where they live or their income
Reduces biomass used by 90% and Slashes emissions by 98+%
